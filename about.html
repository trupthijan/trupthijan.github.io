About Me

Dr. M. Trupthi is an Associate Professor in the Department of Artificial Intelligence at Anurag University, Hyderabad. She received her Ph.D. in Computer Science & Engineering from JNTU Hyderabad in 2020. With over 20 years of teaching experience, she has taught at CBIT, VITS, and JBIET before joining AU. Her research interests include Machine Learning, NLP, Data Science, Blockchain applications, and AI in healthcare.

As a committed educator and IEEE CIS Faculty Advisor, she integrates project‑based learning, mentors student hackathons, and leads outcome‑based curriculum development. Dr. Trupthi has published extensively in top journals and conferences, holds several patents, and actively engages in community outreach and faculty development.
